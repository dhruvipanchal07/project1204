Digital Marketing

Duration: 12 Months


A digital marketing syllabus typically covers a range of topics from foundational marketing principles to specialized areas like SEO, social media, and email marketing. It also includes elements like web analytics, content creation, and online advertising strategies. 

       
 I. Foundational Marketing Principles:


Introduction to Digital Marketing:


    

  • Defining digital marketing, its evolution, and the significance of online strategies. 
    • 



Marketing Basics:


    
    
  • Understanding key marketing concepts, terminology, and the different types of marketing.
    • 

 

Digital Marketing Landscape:


    
    
  • Analyzing the current digital environment, trends, and competitive landscape. 
    • 



Understanding Consumer Behavior:


    
    
  • Studying online consumer behavior, digital marketing strategies, and the consumer decision journey. 
    • 



II. Core Digital Marketing Areas:


Search Engine Optimization (SEO):


     
  • Keyword research, on-page and off-page optimization, technical SEO, and Google Search Console. 
    • 



Search Engine Marketing (SEM):


     
  • Understanding and utilizing paid advertising platforms like Google Ads. 
    • 



Social Media Marketing (SMM): 


  • Creating and managing social media campaigns, content strategy, advertising, and analytics. 
    • 



Content Marketing:


     
  • Developing and distributing valuable content to attract and engage audiences. 
    • 



Email Marketing:
  •  Building email lists, creating effective campaigns, and measuring results. 
    • 



Web Analytics:
     
  • Tracking website traffic, analyzing user behavior, and using data to optimize strategies. 
    • 



Affiliate Marketing:
  •  Understanding the affiliate marketing model and how to leverage it. 
    • 



Online Reputation Management:
  •  Monitoring and managing online reviews and feedback. 
    •

  •  Leveraging influencers to promote products and services. 
    • 

    Marketing & Sales Automation: 
    

  • Using automation tools to streamline marketing and sales processes. 
    • 

    Growth Hacking: 
    

  • Implementing innovative and quick-growth strategies. 
    • 

    AI in Digital Marketing: 
    

  • Exploring the use of artificial intelligence in digital marketing. 
    • 

    Marketing and sales automation:
    

  •  Tools and strategies for streamlining workflows.
